Your Partner in Quality Label Solutions

Introduction

At J.N. Trading Est., we offer a comprehensive suite of label solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of each brand. Whether it’s a high-end woven label, a vibrant screen print, or a durable digital label, we have the expertise to deliver.

Printed Labels

Our printed labels are crafted with precision, offering high-definition colors and lasting durability. Ideal for branding and instructional labels on apparel, accessories, and more.

Woven Labels

Made with attention to detail, our woven labels add a touch of luxury to your products. Choose from a variety of threads and textures for a sophisticated, long-lasting finish.

Embroidery

Add depth and texture to your designs with our custom embroidery services. Perfect for logos and designs requiring a tactile, elegant finish.

Screen Printing

Our screen printing service offers vibrant, long-lasting prints suitable for a range of materials. Ideal for bold, eye-catching designs.

Digital Printing

Using state-of-the-art digital printing technology, we create vivid, photo-quality prints that stay true to your brand vision.

DTF (Direct to Film) Printing

For precision and durability, our DTF printing process allows for high-resolution prints that are perfect for intricate designs and multi-color labels.

Satin Labels

Soft and smooth labels, perfect for adding a touch of luxury to your brand.

Taffeta Labels

Durable and affordable labels, ideal for long-lasting identification

Wash Care Labels

Customizable labels with essential care instructions to keep garments looking their best.

Uniforms

High-quality, comfortable uniforms tailored to meet professional standards.

Custom Solutions

Need something unique? We offer bespoke solutions to ensure your labels are exactly as envisioned.
